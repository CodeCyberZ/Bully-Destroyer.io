How It Works?

Website focus?

Our website focuses on how we can help the victims of bullying—ICP Meycauayan students. It ensures a safe and confidential space where they can freely express their concerns without fear or embarrassment.

Users

The users can report and discuss the bullying incident anonymously through our website. Their concerns will be kept confidential and protected, as they have the right to their privacy.

Chatbot

It is programmed to provide the necessary assistance to the users—victims of bullying. Its knowledge and capabilities were limited, which means it can't provide 100% solution to bullying.

School

Contact information for the school and faculty members is provided in case the chatbot is unable to offer the appropriate assistance. They can contact their school teachers to discuss their concerns and problems more efficiently.

Admin

The admin will monitor the users' usage of the website. They will check the chats of the users since they're the programmer and producer of the website. They're watching and observing the users in case they need the assistance of the admin.

About Us

Bully Destroyer is an anti-bullying chatbot designed to help and support victims of bullying anonymously. Victims will not have to worry about their concerns and issues, as we will ensure their anonymity. Our objective is to keep their information private, secure, and confidential. Our system is a chatbot that interacts with users to discuss their concerns. This system will provide initial assistance before they engage in a formal discussion with their school teachers regarding their problems. The system is a simple rule-based chatbot developed to assist users. It has only limited knowledge and functionality since it follows predefined rules and decision trees. Its responses are highly dependent on the users' inputs and requests. Therefore, we are certain that the chatbot cannot completely resolve all of the users' concerns. In that case, we have provided the school's contact information, allowing you to reach out to the school and faculty members for the necessary support. We are dedicated to providing the best quality service possible, ensuring that every user feels heard, supported, and valued.
